Dice Poker

How to Play

The objective of Dice Poker is to beat the computer to 100 points by rolling the dice and landing on high value poker hands. The game consists of five six-sided-die that, when rolled, combine to form a poker hand. Points are assigned based on the probability of rolling each hand.

The hand rankings, along with their respective points, are detailed below:

  • Straight (Numbers in sequence, like 1-2-3-4-5): 80 points
  • Five of a Kind (All dice showing the same number): 40 points
  • Full House (Three of one number and two of another): 24 points
  • Four of a Kind (Four dice showing the same number): 18 points
  • Three of a Kind (Three dice showing the same number): 8 points
  • Two Pair (Two sets of two dice showing the same number): 5 points
  • One Pair (Two dice showing the same number): 2 points
  • High Card (No pairs): 0 points
